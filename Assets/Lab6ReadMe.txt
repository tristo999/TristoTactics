Simple RPG Featuring a party of three adventurers and six Goblins. Map is made by 32x32 pixel tiles and a quad linked list to represent a grid graph that graph theory 
can be applied to. Map tiles can be passable or not. Using Djikstra's to move and BFS to detect range and attack range. Goal is to eliminate all opponents. 
When attacking hit and misses can be seen in debug console. 

Controls:
WASD - Move Camera
Escape or Menu Button - Open Pause Menu
Mouse Left Click to Interact
